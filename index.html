About Us

The AIML Club at NFSU Gandhinagar is your gateway to the world of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. We bring together curious minds to learn, build, and innovate through hands-on workshops, expert talks, hackathons, and collaborative projects. Whether you're just starting out or looking to deepen your skills, our club is the perfect place to connect, grow, and make an impact in AI/ML.

  • Interactive sessions with industry leaders
  • Real-world projects & competitions
  • Peer learning and study groups
  • Exclusive resources and certifications

Our Journey

Club Launch (Sep 2024)

AIML Club is born! We kicked off our journey to unite AI/ML enthusiasts at NFSU, opening doors to learning, collaboration, and innovation for all students.

DataCamp Access (Dec 2024)

We partnered with DataCamp to give every NFSU student free access to top-tier AI, ML, and Data Science courses—empowering hands-on learning for all skill levels.

Intro Event & CTF (Jan 2025)

Our first event brought 200+ students together for an AI/ML CTF, sparking curiosity, teamwork, and a passion for real-world problem solving in AI.
